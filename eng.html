Группа «Настя» — индивидуальное задание (ответное письмо)

Hi, I’m Nastya.

Thank you for your letter. I liked reading about your school rules.
Now I want to tell you about my school.

In my school we also have some rules.
First of all, we must listen to the teacher and be on time for the lessons.
During the lesson we must be quiet and raise our hand if we want to answer.
We must not shout at the lesson or use mobile phones.

We have homework almost every day and we try to do it well.
We also must keep the classrooms clean and not fight at school.

I think school rules are important because they help us study better and feel comfortable at school.

Best wishes,
Nastya
