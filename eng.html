English. Dialogue: Booking Theatre Tickets

Receptionist: Theatre Royal Haymarket. Good afternoon. How can I help you?

Customer: Hello! I’d like to book some tickets.

Receptionist: Sure. What performance are you interested in?

Customer: Romeo and Juliet, for Saturday, the 28th of September.

Receptionist: How many seats would you like to book?

Customer: Two tickets, please.

Receptionist: Each ticket costs £20. We also have a 10% discount for students.

Customer: That’s perfect. I’ll take them.

Receptionist: The total is £40. How would you like to pay?

Customer: I’ll pay by card.

Receptionist: Thank you. You can pick up your tickets before the show. The performance begins at 8 p.m.

Customer: Thanks a lot!
