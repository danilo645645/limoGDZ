Группа «Настя»

Задание: заполнить письмо (школьные правила).

Dear friend,
In this letter I will try to tell you about the rules I have in my school.
I go to school every day and I have many friends there. I like school a lot and I am ready to spend there even more time than I do. My school has some rules that every pupil has to follow.

First of all we have to listen to the teacher.
If you want to study well and have good marks, you have to be on time at all the lessons.
During the lesson you must be very attentive and raise your hand if you have something to say. Never shout at the lesson!
Sometimes we have homework to do.

There is also dress code in our school. We must wear school uniform. It’s correct I think. We have to look like pupils, not like models.

We have many pupils at school and the corridors at school during the breaks are full. We must not shout and run.
We always have a teacher on duty to keep an eye on us.
Pupils mustn’t use mobile phones at school.
We must keep the classes clean and not fight at school.

As you can see we don’t have many rules at school and it’s not hard to follow them. These rules can teach us to be patient and organized. This may help us in future.

What school rules do you have in your school? Do your students follow the rules? Do you shout at school?
Write back to tell me all about.

Best wishes,

Группа «Маша»
